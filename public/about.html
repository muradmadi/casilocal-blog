About CasiLocal

Who We Are

CasiLocal is a Madrid-based blog created by students, for students. We understand the challenges of navigating a new city and finding the best places to eat, drink, and have fun on a student budget.

Our Mission

Our mission is to help students discover the vibrant culture and amazing spots that Madrid has to offer. From budget-friendly restaurants to the best bars for a night out, from cultural events to hidden gems, we cover everything that makes student life in Madrid exciting.

What We Cover

  • Restaurants: Affordable dining options, student-friendly spots, and local favorites
  • Bars: The best places to grab a drink, from cozy pubs to trendy cocktail bars
  • Events: Cultural events, festivals, concerts, and activities perfect for students
  • Tips & Guides: Practical advice for making the most of your time in Madrid
