Madrid's nightlife is legendary, and as a student, you want to experience it without emptying your wallet. We've compiled a list of the best student-friendly bars where you can enjoy Madrid's vibrant bar scene.

## Rooftop Bars

### 1. El Jardín Secreto

Located in the heart of Malasaña, this rooftop bar offers stunning views of the city. While drinks can be a bit pricier, they have student nights on Thursdays with discounted prices.

**Price Range:** €6-10 per drink  
**Best For:** Sunset drinks and city views

## Cozy Neighborhood Bars

### 2. La Vía Láctea

A classic Malasaña bar that's been a student favorite for decades. The atmosphere is relaxed, the music is great, and the prices are unbeatable.

**Price Range:** €3-6 per drink  
**Best For:** Casual drinks with friends

### 3. El Tigre

Famous for their generous free tapas with every drink, El Tigre is a must-visit for budget-conscious students. Order a caña (small beer) and you'll get a plate of delicious tapas included.

**Price Range:** €3-5 per drink (with free tapas!)  
**Best For:** Getting the most bang for your buck

## Craft Beer Spots

### 4. Fábrica Maravillas

If you're a craft beer enthusiast, this is the place for you. Located in Malasaña, they brew their own beer and offer a great selection at reasonable prices.

**Price Range:** €4-7 per beer  
**Best For:** Craft beer lovers

## Tips for Bar Hopping in Madrid

1. **Start early** – Many bars offer "happy hour" specials in the early evening (usually 6-9 PM).

2. **Look for "caña" deals** – A caña (small beer) is usually the cheapest option and often comes with free tapas.

3. **Visit during weekdays** – Weekday nights are generally less crowded and sometimes have better deals.
